EFT, also known as tapping, first came to prominence in the 1990s when developer Gary Craig published his findings on the therapy on his website (https://eftinternational.org/discover-eft-tapping/what-is-eft-tapping/). It involves tapping specific points on the body, primarily on the head and the face, in a particular sequence. While doing this, the person focuses on the issue that they wish to treat. EFT Tapping helps us tune in to the negative patterns that we form around our uncomfortable thoughts, feelings, or troubling memories. We ‘tap’ on the pressure points while bringing the thoughts or emotions into consciousness. The aim is to find relief, relaxation, and promote healing around the emotional or physical issues that hold us back.
